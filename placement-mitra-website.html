Who We Are

Placement Mitra is a Delhi NCR-based hiring consultancy founded in 2018 with a mission to make recruitment simpler, faster, and more human. We work across industries — from IT, Pharmaceutical and finance to manufacturing, FMCG, and more.

Our team of experienced recruiters understands that hiring is not just about CVs and job descriptions. It's about people, culture fit, and long-term growth. That's why we take a consultative approach to every mandate we handle.

Whether you're a startup looking for your first 10 employees or an enterprise scaling your workforce, Placement Mitra is your reliable talent partner. We bring urgency, quality, and transparency to every search.
